Screen Walkthrough

Login & Authentication

The login page validates credentials before granting access. Wrong email or password shows an error — the form stays until valid credentials are entered.

Login page with email/password fields and demo account quick-fill buttons at the bottom.
  1. Open https://dsr-fortune-prime.vercel.app in your browser.
  2. Enter your Email and Password (or click a demo account to auto-fill).
  3. Click the green "Sign In" button.
  4. On success, you are redirected to the Dashboard.
Error state — "Invalid email or password" displayed for incorrect credentials.

My Profile

Personal information & settings

The profile page shows your account details: editable Personal Information, Change Password, and read-only Facility Information.

My Profile — edit display name, email, phone; change password; view facility details (DSR Fortune Prime, Residential, Bangalore).
  • Personal Information — Edit display name, email, phone. Role is read-only. Click "Save Changes" to update.
  • Change Password — Enter current password, new password (min 6 chars), confirm. Click "Update Password".
  • Facility Information — Read-only: facility name, type, city, client.
  • Sign Out — Available from profile header, sidebar, or profile dropdown (3 locations).
All 10 Modules

Every screen mapped

Below is a walkthrough of each module with screenshots captured from the live production portal via automated browser testing.

1. Daily Updates

Primary data entry for facility staff. 8 tabs: Update (power/water readings), Hygiene, Power, Water, Complaints, Tasks, Projects, Vendor tickets. Enter current meter readings and click "Save Power" or "Save Water".

Update tab — Power Readings (EB & DG meters) and Water Readings entry forms.

2. Attendance & Staffing

Workforce management with 9 tabs: Employees, Manual Entry, Attendance, QR Scanner, Staff & Shift, Shift View, Calendar View, Billing, Timesheet. Supports Excel/PDF export and bulk upload.

Employee list — name, ID, mobile, email, designation, QR code status, edit/delete actions.

3. Asset Management

Track every physical asset across 9 categories. 8 tabs: Categories, Checklists, Dashboard, AMC Contracts, Breakdown/RCA, Gate Pass, 52-Week PPM, Asset Audit (QR-based scanning).

Asset Categories — 9 categories (Earthing Pits, HVAC, Fire Safety, etc.) with View/Add/Upload per category.

4. Facility Configuration

Set up facility details, power meters, water infrastructure, water quality parameters, hygiene templates, shifts, and service providers. 4 tabs: Facility Config, Daily Update, Employee, Service Provider.

Facility Configuration with tabs for facility details, daily update config, employee settings, and service providers.

5. Alerts & Monitoring

Auto-generated alerts from facility data. Click "Refresh & Generate" to scan attendance, asset audits, water quality, tasks, and AMC contracts. Severity-coded: Critical (red), High (orange), Medium (yellow), Low (blue). Actions: Acknowledge, Dismiss, Resolve.

Alerts page — auto-generated alerts grouped by category with severity badges and action buttons.

6. Alert Configuration

Configure categories, attendance/maintenance thresholds, MLSS water quality parameters, audit deadline reminders, and notification channels (Email, SMS, WhatsApp, Push) with per-role toggles.

Configuration tab — master toggle, category toggles, thresholds, MLSS parameters, notification channels.

7. Reports & KPIs

KPI Traffic Light Dashboard with 18 metrics across 6 categories (Attendance, Operations, Utilities, Maintenance, Quality, Safety). Green = on target, Red = below. Includes Analytics tab and downloadable PDF reports.

KPI Traffic Light Dashboard — 18 KPIs with values vs targets, plus 6 category progress bars.

8. AI Agent

Conversational AI assistant powered by Google Gemini. Ask questions like "How many complaints are open?", "Which assets have overdue maintenance?", "Generate a water consumption summary for April."

AI Agent chat interface — type questions, receive AI-powered facility insights.

9. User Management

Full CRUD: Create, Edit, Change Role, Activate/Deactivate, Delete users. Searchable table with role/status filters. Click any role name to change it. All actions are logged in the audit trail.

User list — name, phone, email, role (clickable), status, creation date, three-dot action menu.

10. Roles & Permissions

Permission matrix: 11 roles x 9 permissions. Green checkmark = granted, gray X = denied. Admin role is locked. Click any cell to toggle. Purple badges show user counts per role.

Role-permission matrix — Admin, Client, Facility Manager, Guest, Manager, Supervisor, and more.

11. Audit Log

Chronological log of all user actions: logins, user creation, edits, role changes, deletions. Color-coded action badges. Searchable and paginated (10 per page).

Audit log — timestamp, user, action badge, details, and IP address for each event.

12. Biometric Devices

Register ZKTeco / eSSL biometric terminals, map device users to employees, review unmapped punches. 3 tabs: Devices, Enrollments, Unmapped Queue.

Biometric Devices — device registration with Refresh and "+ Add Device" buttons.
